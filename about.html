About GlobeNews

GlobeNews is your go-to platform for accessing the latest news from around the world with an interactive and engaging experience.

Experience the World of News in 3D

GlobeNews offers a unique approach to news delivery by allowing users to explore news stories on a 3D model of the Earth. Click on a country, and the latest headlines will appear, providing you with real-time updates from across the globe.

Our mission is to make news more accessible and engaging, breaking down barriers and bringing stories from every corner of the world right to your fingertips.
